# Business Insights

## Business Questions

1. Which courses generate the highest revenue?
2. What customer profile purchases the most?
3. Which sales representatives achieve the best performance?
4. How have sales evolved over time?
5. Which factors appear to have the greatest influence on sales?

---

## 1. Which courses generate the highest revenue?

Based on the analysis, the courses that generated the highest revenue for CCS between 2024 and 2026 are:

| Course | Students | Total Revenue (COP) |
|---------|---------:|--------------------:|
| Sexual Violence | 1,146 | $33,402,714.97 |
| ACLS | 323 | $29,765,760.00 |
| BLS | 888 | $26,191,001.97 |
| Humanization | 863 | $25,476,834.97 |
| Chemical Safety | 748 | $21,920,269.97 |

The most consistent courses across the three years (2024–2026) are:

1. Sexual Violence
2. Chemical Safety

Although ACLS has fewer enrollments than the other courses, it generates one of the highest revenues due to its higher average ticket price, approximately **COP $94,000 per student**.

---

## 2. What customer profile purchases the most?

The analysis shows that the customer segment generating the highest revenue for CCS between 2024 and 2026 has the following characteristics:

- Female customers
- Between 30 and 50 years old
- Mainly located in Pasto
- Primarily acquired through Facebook
- Preferred payment methods: Nequi and Bancolombia

## 4. How have sales evolved over time?

The analysis indicates a clear upward trend in sales between 2024 and 2026.

Key findings include:

- Sales consistently peak during **January, May, and July**.
- Total revenue in **2025 exceeded the results obtained in 2024**.
- By **July 2026**, accumulated sales were already close to matching the total revenue generated during the entire year of 2024.

Overall, the business demonstrates sustained growth over the analyzed period.

---

## 5. Which factors appear to have the greatest influence on sales?

The analysis suggests that the following factors have the strongest impact on business performance:

1. **Corporate contracts** generate the largest monthly fluctuations in revenue, contributing an average of approximately **COP $2.7 million** in additional monthly income.

2. **Seasonality** plays an important role, with January consistently showing the highest sales volume each year. After January, monthly sales stabilize at an average of approximately **COP $5.2 million**.

3. **Sales representatives** provide a steady contribution throughout the year, generating an average monthly revenue of approximately **COP $1.8 million**.

4. Approximately **72% of CCS customers are women**, while **28% are men**, indicating that female customers represent the primary market segment.

5. **January has historically been the strongest sales month** for both the CCS Pasto branch and its sales representatives.

# Key Business Takeaways

- The **Sexual Violence** course is the company's primary revenue driver.
- **ACLS** has the highest average revenue per student, making it the most profitable course.
- The primary customer segment consists of **women aged 30–50** located in Pasto.
- Sales show a consistent upward trend, with **January** representing the strongest month each year.
- Corporate contracts are a major contributor to monthly revenue growth and business performance.
